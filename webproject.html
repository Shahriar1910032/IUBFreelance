Web Project

Location: Independent University Bangladesh

Develop a web application similar to Fiverr or Upwork, designed to facilitate seamless transactions between buyers and sellers of services.

Key Responsibilities:

    A marketplace web application like Fiverr or Upwork allows freelancers to offer their services and clients to hire them for various projects. The platform connects freelancers with potential clients by listing different types of jobs or services (writing, design, programming, etc.). Clients can browse freelancer profiles, check reviews, and post job requirements, while freelancers can submit proposals and showcase their skills. Essential features include user registration, profile creation, service listing, job posting, secure payment processing, messaging, and a review system for both freelancers and clients. The web app ensures seamless interaction, trust, and security for both parties.

Requirements:

  • Proven experience as a Digital Marketing Specialist or similar role
  • Strong understanding of digital marketing channels (SEO, PPC, Social Media)
  • Familiarity with web analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Excellent communication and project management skills
  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business, or related field
